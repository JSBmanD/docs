Branch combines every deep linking standard into a single, simple package. Easy to implement, and always up to date.

A Branch link is an actual page on the web. When users click a Branch link, they open that webpage, and we get pinged. We use matching to detect your usersâ€™ device, operating system, and browser, and combine that with cookies to either remember or check whether they have the app installed. Then we can redirect them for an optimal experience.
